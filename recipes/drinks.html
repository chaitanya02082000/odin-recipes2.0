Hot and Sweet Ginger Drink

Description

This old Chinese ginger drink recipe is deceptively simple but addictive. Great substitute for hot spiced apple cider during the holidays. Warms the soul on a cold winter's evening!

Ingredients

  • 5 cups water
  • Â¼ cup sliced fresh ginger
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Steps

  1. Bring water and ginger to a boil in a saucepan. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer for 5 minutes.
  2. Stir in brown sugar and honey until sugar has dissolved. Strain out ginger to serve.
